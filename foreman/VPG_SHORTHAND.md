## J ownership boundary

J authorizes attribution, verification, commit, and branch push for work created by the current Heimerdinker/helper cycle. It does not sweep unrelated Ben-owned or historical dirty files into a commit, promote Production, merge branches, open providers, mutate data, reveal secrets, or clear a separately named Human Gate unless the Operator explicitly says so.

## Completion language

`SENT`, `DELIVERED`, `OPENED`, `CLAIMED`, and `PUSHED` are intermediate states. Finish with `COMPLETED` or a specific `BLOCKER`.
