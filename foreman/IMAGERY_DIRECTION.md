## Arc shift — "Anyone can be anything" (2026-06-08)

Prior passes ran on "people can change." Current arc runs on **anyone can be anything** — bolder, easier to get wrong. The credibility rule applies to every frame:

- Never show possibility without **concrete, reachable means**.
- **Real ordinary people** make "anyone" believable (not hoodie founders).
- **Closer/cheaper than you thought** reveals make "anything" believable (credit union yes, used oven in budget).

### Five UX beats (distinct from four-act Spark / Space / Forge / Foundry renders)

| Beat | Feeling | Imagery priority |
|------|---------|------------------|
| **Lost** | Understood, not reduced | One person, unnamed need, dim industrial-real |
| **Searching** | Maybe not what I came for | Same person, off-path notice; Squibb notices |
| **Discovery** | Real need — reachable | Door; affordable oven; lender handshake |
| **Formation** | Safe to act, not alone | Verified partner / lender / seller |
| **Momentum** | Beginning, more to become | Running business; same person further along |

### Discovery categories (shot weight)

Give **Money** and **Equipment** extra weight — they turn dreams into numbers a real person can hit.

- **People** — partner, mentor, friend at kitchen table
- **Money** — credit union, local lender (not VC)
- **Space** — place to build, within reach
- **Equipment** — used commercial gear, cheaper than assumed

## Emotional job

The viewer should feel like they are stepping into **the chapter where they finally become the person making the decisions**.

Not:

- watching someone else’s movie
- being sold a lifestyle fantasy
- joining a cult of confidence

Yes:

- **protagonist energy** — they are in the frame, not applauding from the aisle
- **professional credibility** — real work, real stakes, real tools
- **Werkle formation** — incomplete alone → credible together

## Express through

| Device | How to use it |
|--------|----------------|
| **Human lead portraits** | One clear lead per frame. Direct gaze or purposeful side profile. Composed, not performing. |
| **Lane-based profile cards** | Builder, Operator, Backer, Connector, Spark — each with distinct props and posture, same visual language. |
| **Pairing / formation** | Show **incomplete → complete**: solo with gap → two or three people + shared artifact (blueprint, contract stack, sample on table). |
| **Props of agency** | Notes, plans, tools, contracts, blueprints, product samples, clipboards, laptops closed/not glowing heroically. |
| **Believable spaces** | Workshop, job site edge, small office, garage bench, café table with papers — **heightened realism**, not stylized sets. |
| **Cinematic composition, grounded** | Strong foreground/midground, motivated light, shallow depth — but still a place you could walk into. |
| **Cast + formation structure** | Roster of credible people and roles; **small ensemble moments** — not giant narrative dioramas. |

## Hard bans (cringe filters)

| Ban | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Literal **movie-poster** art | No title-card staging, no epic sky backdrops, no “team assembled for battle” poses. |
| **Fantasy** | No swords, crowns, dragons, magical forge, RPG lighting, medieval workshop cosplay. |
| **Stock-photo corporate** | No handshake-in-sunset, no multiethnic boardroom pointing at blank chart, no fake laughter at laptop. |
| **Melodrama** | No tears, fist pumps, arms raised in victory, villain silhouettes, romance-cover intensity. |
| **Guru fog** | No mentor on mountain, no halo light on speaker, no “I will change your life” energy. |
| **Luxury posturing** | No watches-as-hero, no champagne, no penthouse-as-identity. |

If it feels like **advertisement for confidence** instead of **evidence of work**, reject it.

## Lane visual cues (cast, not costume)

Same world, different props — not five unrelated stock archetypes.

| Lane | Posture | Prop hints | Avoid |
|------|---------|------------|-------|
| **Builder** | Hands on material or tool | Sample part, fab table, marked-up sketch | Hard hat as costume only |
| **Operator** | Calm control, checklist energy | Schedule board, keys, route map, vendor folder | “CEO standing alone” cliché |
| **Backer** | Present, not throne-seated | Term sheet redlines, cap table printout, sober folder | Wolf-of-wall energy |
| **Connector** | Between people or objects | Intro note, phone face-down, two chairs angled | Networking-event grin |
| **Spark** | Leaning in, not manic | Napkin sketch, photo of opportunity, half-built mock | “Lightbulb moment” stock |

Squibb (mascot) stays **host/guide scale** — not the human protagonist. See `foreman/MASCOT_RULES.md`.

## Formation grammar (incomplete → complete)

Use **three-beat visual logic**, not a storyboard:

1. **Solo + gap** — one person, empty chair, second coffee, unanswered message on desk.
2. **Aligned artifacts** — blueprint/contract/plan spans two sides of table.
3. **Locked joints** — two or three people, same document in frame, complementary lanes visible.

Do **not** illustrate the whole product journey in one image. One beat per asset.

## Transformation & formation (identity shift without morphing)

**Core idea:** People are not trapped in their current identity. Lanes can combine into a **Werkle** — a working machine locked from complementary roles.

**Viability (recorded 2026-05-31):** Direction is **viable** with restrained visual grammar.

**How to imply transformation — allowed:**

| Beat | Device |
|------|--------|
| Identity shift | Same anonymous lead, **prop/context change** (trade tools → operator folder → shared blueprint) — triptych or card stack |
| Lane choice | Five lane cards; posture + props change, not costume or face morph |
| Unlikely collaboration | Two trades, mismatched tools, **one shared plan** on the table |
| Werkle formation | 2–3 people + shared artifact + complementary props — “locked joints” |

**Transformation — forbidden:**

- Literal **magical morphing**, face/body AI transform, cartoon squash-stretch
- RPG “class change” UI, fantasy game art
- Copyrighted characters, celebrity likenesses
- Banking / fintech stock tropes

**Motion (when approved later):** restrained loops only — prop crossfade, second chair, document slid across table. **`prefers-reduced-motion` → static.** Never autoplay heavy video on proof/billing/login.

**Static first:** prove grammar on `/#people`, `/#how`, `/onboarding` before any motion spend.

See `foreman/ghost-forge/IMAGERY_PROMPT_TEMPLATE.md` for Ghost Forge tier (Gate 05 **PAUSE**).

## Composition notes

- **Foreground prop** anchors agency (pen on contract, calipers on part, stamped blueprint).
- **Midground human** carries protagonist energy — waist-up or environmental portrait.
- **Background** supports place (workshop depth, window light, tool wall) — never steals focus.
- **Light:** warm practicals (work lamp, window, forge glow as **atmosphere scrim** per site style v0.6) — not supernatural rim light.
- **Color:** copper frame, warm paper, forge-orange as **environment only** — not CTA orange on people’s faces.
