The content is organized into eight specific pillars, designed to attack the exact gaps left by competitors like BiggerPockets and Codie Sanchez.

## 1. Partnership Hygiene

* **Core Question:** How do we structure this without destroying each other?
* **Focus:** Operating agreements, equity splits, vesting, role clarity, dissolution triggers.
* **Products:** Operating Agreement Starter Template ($97 draft), Equity Split Calculator ($47 draft).

## 2. Self-Protection

* **Core Question:** How do I protect myself before something goes wrong?
* **Focus:** LLC structure, piercing the corporate veil, personal guarantees, non-compete traps.
* **Products:** Entity Comparison Checklist ($29 draft), Vendor Contract Checklist ($47 draft).

## 3. Real Estate Realism

* **Core Question:** Does the math actually work, and where does it break?

## 4. Investing vs. Buying a Job

* **Core Question:** Is what I'm buying a business or an expensive W-2?

* **Products:** Business Freedom Audit ($29 draft), SDE Adjustment Worksheet ($67 draft).

## 5. Money Foundations
