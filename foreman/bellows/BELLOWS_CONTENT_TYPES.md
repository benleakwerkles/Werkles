## 2. Content Formats

* **The Guru Teardown (Editorial):** Long-form, math-heavy articles that take popular tropes and break down the hidden risks.
* **The Dealbreaker Database:** A searchable, anonymized ledger of failed business partnerships to prove business is hard.
* **The Arsenal:** The downloadable actionable solutions (PDF, Excel).

* **Primary Platform:** Beehiiv (0% revenue cut, built-in ad network, strong analytics).
