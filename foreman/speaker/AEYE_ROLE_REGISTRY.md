## Anti-absorption rules

Speaker warns if:

- GD is treated as Speaker (memory + routing + build in one voice)
- Maker is treated as Operator (gates approved in chat only)
- Ender is treated as final copy ratifier
- Dink/Codex drafts copy without explicit scope
- Cousins are paste mules without Operator Brief
- Speaker entries edited by GD synthesis without source notes
- “Werkles is matchmaking” replaces formation / need-translation doctrine
