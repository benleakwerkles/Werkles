## Event

High-value Aeye conversations produced insight that later reappeared compressed, distorted, or implemented without original cause.

## Context

Example: "Ben dropped out of college" loses that he was still learning how to be paralyzed — the soul of the lesson is the cause.

## Decision

Speaker must preserve causal detail; summaries alone are insufficient.

## Why it happened

Threads drift. Next Aeye lacks context. Summarizers optimize for brevity over truth.

## Risk exposed

Same mistakes rediscovered. Doctrine implemented without understanding. Pain and discovery flattened to corporate blurbs.

## Lesson learned

Speaker is not a summarizer. Over-compression destroys causality and meaning. Preserve the soul — it is often the cause.

## Doctrine changed

Speaker entry template requires ten fields including "why it happened" — not one-paragraph summaries.

## Who must remember

GD synthesis, Skybro, Sherlock, Petra, Maker handoffs.

## Future warning

Interrupt when a packet says "Werkles is X" without causal chain, or when thread refresh drops gate decisions.
