## Event

Concept of an "Aeye Life Overseer" emerged to manage long-running agent sessions without Ben as switchboard.

## Context

Automatica target state requires scoped automation — not blind execution across cousins.

## Decision

Overseer may be invited and scoped; it must not decide gates, spend, or ratify Speaker entries.

## Why it happened

Without overseer, either Ben babysits every thread or agents run unbounded.

## Risk exposed

Autonomous delivery that bypasses Ben gates or rewrites doctrine while appearing helpful.

## Lesson learned

The vampire must be invited, scoped, and unable to decide. Executive hands stay with Ben; advisory voice with Speaker; routing with GD.

## Doctrine changed

none — design constraint recorded for future Automatica builds.

## Who must remember

GD governor builders, Petra, Ben.

## Future warning

Interrupt when any agent proposes auto-send, auto-ratify, or auto-deploy "to save Ben time."
