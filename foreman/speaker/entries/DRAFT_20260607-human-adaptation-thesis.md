## Event

Multiple Aeyes (GD, cousins, Maker) operated across threads and Edge tabs while missions ended at repo artifacts without Operator-readable briefs.

## Context

Werkles runs on Sally with cockpit files, cousin packets, and chat threads that each compress context on handoff.

## Decision

Establish Speaker as independent office and human-first output rules (`HUMAN_CONSUMABLE_OUTPUT_RULE_V1`).

## Why it happened

Systems preserved events (commits, packets) but lost meaning (why Ben wanted the change, what gate applied). Ben became the courier between tools.

## Risk exposed

Founder intent thins. Gates blur with IDE prompts. Operator labor returns through paste and path hunting.

## Lesson learned

People are not static. People **become**. Werkles must adapt to how Ben decides — not force Ben to adapt to agent file trees. Every lane must answer: can Ben take the next step without opening repo files?

## Doctrine changed

Ben is Operator, not manual labor or copy/paste transport. Speaker is constitutionally separate from GD.

## Who must remember

GD, Maker, Petra, Foreman dispatch, all cousin seats.

## Future warning

Interrupt when builds change routing/relay without ledger check, or when "done" means artifacts queued but Operator cannot act.
