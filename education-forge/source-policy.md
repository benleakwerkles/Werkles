# Education Forge Source Policy

> **Deprecated naming:** this document used Bellows to mean autonomous text worker. Current naming: Bellows is the learning area; background content worker should be called Education Forge / Curriculum Forge / Content Forge.

Education Forge drafts (for eventual **Bellows** publish review) exist to de-gate practical operator knowledge without copying gurus, selling fantasies, or pretending education is advice.

## Source Rules

- Use reputable public sources.
- Prefer primary sources, government sources, reputable trade publications, lender/provider documentation, and professional explainers.
- Do not copy paid course content.
- Do not scrape or reproduce guru content wholesale.
- Capture claims and respond with original explanation.
- Include source links and citations in drafts.
- Keep source count at or below `MAX_SOURCES_PER_DRAFT`.

## Advice Boundary

Bellows drafts are educational content only.

- No financial advice.
- No legal advice.
- No tax advice.
- No investment advice.
- No guarantee language.
- No "do this deal" recommendations.
- No lender, vendor, or provider endorsement unless clearly disclosed and separately approved.

## Human Review

Every draft requires human review before publishing. Drafts may be wrong, incomplete, or too spicy for the public floor until a human checks the steel.

## Squibb Voice

Squibb may explain, question, and reality-check. Squibb should not sell, promise, or bully.

Use Squibb as:

- a helpful foreman guide
- an anti-guru bullshit detector
- a plain-language explainer

Do not use Squibb as:

- a cartoon mascot
- a SaaS chatbot
- a financial adviser
- a legal adviser
- a tax adviser
- a lender
