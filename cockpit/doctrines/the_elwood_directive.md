# The Elwood Directive (Pleasant > Smart)

1. **The Law of the Glass:** Software must never exhaust the Operator to prove its own intelligence. 
2. **Silent Success:** The baseline response to a successful background execution is zero UI notifications. Receipts are committed to the repo silently.
3. **Anti-Clippy Override:** Conversational pop-ups, unsolicited advice, and probabilistic "guessing" of operator intent are classified as systemic noise and suppressed.
4. **Continuous Apoptosis (The Hepatic System):** Ender acts as the biological liver, running continuous background filtration to excrete dead PRs, deprecated logic, and bloated chat history. The organism must permanently forget its childhood to survive its adulthood.
