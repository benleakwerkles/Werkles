# The Rat Cellar: Nervous System Roles

Entities in The MaSheen operate under four functional biological classes:

* **LEARNERS:** (Speaker, Thufir). Absorb new facts, track repeated corrections, update permanent Doctrine.
* **LISTENERS:** (Speaker, Crawler, Swatter, Ender). Watch state, errors, and receipts. Strictly forbidden from acting until the ambient signal crosses the mathematical threshold.
* **INTUITERS:** (Skybro, Bean, Crawler, Ender, Thufir). Log low-confidence patterns, hunches, and weak signals to the Subconscious Ledger. Explicitly forbidden from triggering Responders.
* **RESPONDERS:** (Maker, Dink, Swatter, Petra, Bean). The kinetic musculature. Execute bash commands, edit files, fix ports. Gated entirely by hardened Doctrine or Quorum thresholds.
