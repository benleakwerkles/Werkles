# Operator Cockpit

STATUS: DRAFT - HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED

This folder is a repo-based operator map for Ben's separated business entities. It is a coordination layer, not a blended legal, Drive, accounting, banking, or vendor system.

Core rule: one Operator Cockpit with links across separated entities. Do not create one blended legal/Drive/accounting blob.

## Files

- `ENTITY_MAP.md` - entity-by-entity map with purpose, ownership, assets, accounts, vendors, separation rules, and open questions.
- `SHARED_ACCESS_MODEL.md` - how shared access can work without commingling ownership, money, files, or credentials.
- `ENTITY_SEPARATION_RULES.md` - operating rules for keeping entities, accounts, vendors, and money separated.
- `OPEN_LEGAL_CPA_QUESTIONS.md` - questions for Ben, counsel, CPA, and bookkeeper before final structure decisions.

## Operating Rules

- Repo cockpit files are source of truth for coordination notes only.
- Legal formation documents, operating agreements, bank records, tax records, contracts, and provider dashboards remain source of truth for their own domains.
- Do not move Google Drive ownership from this cockpit.
- Do not change account permissions from this cockpit.
- Do not enter, print, or store secrets in this cockpit.
- Do not use this cockpit to approve billing, OAuth, payment, deploy, or final create/share actions.

## Review State
